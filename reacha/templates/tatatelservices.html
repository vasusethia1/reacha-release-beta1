ABOUT THE PROJECT

Samvedna connected school children from across cities through Information and Communication Technology (ICT). Voice and data technology linked Him Jyoti School, Dehra Dun and Manava Bharati India International School, New Delhi to start with. This pilot programme was delivered by REACHA, a Voluntary Organisation, from October 2008 to September 2009 in partnership with Tata Tele Services Limited (TTSL).

CBSE and NCERT emphasise on the need to integrate ICT toolkits in classroom teaching learning. Samvedna did just that and much more!!
